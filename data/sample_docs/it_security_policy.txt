TechCorp Inc.
IT Security Policy (Excerpt)
================================

SECTION 1: PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS
---------------------------------
Passwords must be at least 12 characters and include uppercase, lowercase,
a number, and a symbol. Passwords expire every 90 days.

SECTION 2: VPN ACCESS
---------------------
Remote access requires the company VPN. Install TechCorp VPN from the
software portal. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory.

SECTION 3: DEVICE POLICY
------------------------
Company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled.
Personal devices may not store customer data or source code.

SECTION 4: INCIDENT REPORTING
-----------------------------
Report suspected security incidents to security@techcorp.com within 30 minutes.
Do not delete logs or shut down systems unless instructed by IT Security.

SECTION 6: ACCEPTABLE USE
-------------------------
Work systems are for business use only.
Streaming, torrenting, and unauthorized software are prohibited.
