Green Valley Medical Center
Employee Handbook (Excerpt)
================================

SECTION 4: REMOTE WORK POLICY
-----------------------------
Non-clinical staff (administration, billing, IT support, and quality assurance)
may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

Clinical staff providing direct patient care must be on-site unless approved
for telehealth visits under Section 12.

Remote employees must:
- Use the hospital VPN for all work systems
- Keep patient data off personal devices (HIPAA compliance)
- Be available during core hours: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM Eastern

SECTION 5: PAID TIME OFF (PTO)
------------------------------
Full-time employees accrue PTO as follows:
- 0-2 years of service: 15 days per year
- 3-5 years of service: 20 days per year
- 6+ years of service: 25 days per year

PTO requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance in the HR portal,
except for emergencies.

SECTION 7: HIPAA & DATA SECURITY
--------------------------------
All employees must complete annual HIPAA training by January 31.

Patient information must never be shared via personal email, text message,
or unsecured messaging apps. Report suspected breaches to compliance@gvmed.org
within 1 hour.

SECTION 9: ON-CALL COVERAGE
---------------------------
Clinical on-call rotations are assigned monthly. On-call clinicians must
respond to pages within 15 minutes. Compensation is $150 per 24-hour shift
plus standard overtime rates if called in.

SECTION 11: DRESS CODE
----------------------
Clinical areas: scrubs with hospital ID badge visible at all times.
Administrative areas: business casual Monday-Thursday; casual Friday permitted.
