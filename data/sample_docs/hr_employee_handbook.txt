TechCorp Inc.
HR Employee Handbook (Excerpt)
================================

SECTION 2: REMOTE WORK
------------------------
All full-time employees may work from home up to 2 days per week.
Manager approval is required. Core hours are 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM local time.

SECTION 3: PAID TIME OFF
------------------------
- 0-2 years: 12 days per year
- 3-5 years: 18 days per year
- 6+ years: 24 days per year

PTO must be requested at least 1 week in advance in the HR portal.

SECTION 5: PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
--------------------------------
Reviews happen every 6 months in June and December.
Employees receive written feedback and a rating: Exceeds, Meets, or Needs Improvement.

SECTION 8: BENEFITS
-------------------
Health insurance starts on day 30. The company covers 80% of premiums.
401(k) match is 4% after 90 days of employment.
