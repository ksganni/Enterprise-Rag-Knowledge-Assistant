TechCorp Inc.
Finance & Expense Policy (Excerpt)
================================

SECTION 1: EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
--------------------------------
Submit expense reports within 30 days of purchase via the Finance portal.
Receipts are required for any expense over $25.

SECTION 2: TRAVEL
-----------------
Flights must be booked in economy class unless approved by a director.
Hotel limit is $200 per night in the US, $250 internationally.
Meals while traveling: up to $75 per day.

SECTION 3: CORPORATE CARD
-------------------------
Corporate cards are issued to managers and above.
Personal purchases on a corporate card must be repaid within 14 days.

SECTION 4: BUDGET APPROVAL
--------------------------
Purchases over $5,000 require VP approval.
Purchases over $25,000 require CFO approval.

SECTION 5: NON-REIMBURSABLE ITEMS
---------------------------------
Alcohol, personal entertainment, traffic fines, and gym memberships
are not reimbursable unless part of an approved wellness program.
